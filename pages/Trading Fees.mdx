## FAQ

### How are fees calculated?

Fees are charged per trade, based on the filled notional position size. Fee tiers are calculated based on a rolling 30-day trading volume.

### Can I stack staking discounts and volume tier discounts?

**Yes**, Staking discounts and rebates are in addition to your volume tier.

### Are spot markets included?

This update currently applies to **perpetual futures markets** only.
